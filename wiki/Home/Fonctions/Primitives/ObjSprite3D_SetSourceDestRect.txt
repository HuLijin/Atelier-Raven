====== ObjSprite3D SetSourceDestRect ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) left (real)
 3) top (real)
 4) right (real)
 5) bottom (real)
Sets the texture rectangle for the sprite, and maps the center of the rectangle to the destination coordinates (0, 0).
