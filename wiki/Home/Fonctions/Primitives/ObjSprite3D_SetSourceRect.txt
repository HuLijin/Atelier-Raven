====== ObjSprite3D SetSourceRect ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) texture coordinate (left) (real)
 3) texture coordinate (top) (real)
 4) texture coordinate (right) (real)
 5) texture coordinate (bottom) (real)
Sets the texture rectangle for the sprite.
