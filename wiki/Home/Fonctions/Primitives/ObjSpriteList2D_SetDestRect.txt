====== ObjSpriteList2D SetDestRect ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) left (real)
 3) top (real)
 4) right (real)
 5) bottom (real)
Sets the drawing rectangle for the sprite. This is the rectangle where the sprite will be drawn.
