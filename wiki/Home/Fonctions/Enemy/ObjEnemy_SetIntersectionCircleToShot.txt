====== ObjEnemy SetIntersectionCircleToShot ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) collision detection x-coordinate (real)
 3) collision detection y-coordinate (real)
 4) collision detection radius (real)
Sets the position and size of the enemy hitbox (any attack from the player touching the circle will damage the enemy).
