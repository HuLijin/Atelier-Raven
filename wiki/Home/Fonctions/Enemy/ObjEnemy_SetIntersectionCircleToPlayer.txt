====== ObjEnemy SetIntersectionCircleToPlayer ======

ObjEnemy_SetIntersectionCircleToPlayer

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) collision detection x-coordinate (real)
 3) collision detection y-coordinate (real)
 4) collision detection radius (real)
Sets the enemy hitbox for player collision. Any collision with the circle will kill the character.
