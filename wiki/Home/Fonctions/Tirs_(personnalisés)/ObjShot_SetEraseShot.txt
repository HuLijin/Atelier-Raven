====== ObjShot SetEraseShot ======

Parameters:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) erase (bool)
Enables/disables the ability to erase enemy shots when the specified shot object comes into contact with it. Each time a shot is erased, the penetration of the shot object will go down by 1. __For player shots only.__
