====== ObjCrLaser SetTipDecrement ======

Parameters:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) tip transparency reduction rate (0.0-1.0) (real) 
Sets the transparency reduction rate at the tip of the curved laser object. Default is 1.0 (tip of laser is invisible).
