====== ObjItem SetDefinedMovePatternA1 ======

Parameters:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) movement type (real)
Sets the movement type of the item object. Movement types are:

ITEM_MOVE_DOWN: immediately drops down
ITEM_MOVE_TOPLAYER: automatically flies towards the player
Defaults to flying upwards and then coming down.
