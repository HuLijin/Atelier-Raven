====== SetFogParam ======

Arguments:
 1) starting point (distance from the player)
 2) ending point (distance from the player)
 3) color (R)
 4) color (G)
 5) color (B)
Sets the fog parameters.

To turn the screen dark around the player, you can use SetFogParam(250, 700, 0, 0, 0).
