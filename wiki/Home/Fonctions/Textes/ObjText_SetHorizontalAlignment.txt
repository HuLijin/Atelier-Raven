====== ObjText SetHorizontalAlignment ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) alignment (real)
Sets the alignment of the text. You can choose among:

ALIGNMENT_LEFT
ALIGNMENT_CENTER
ALIGNMENT_RIGHT
To use center or right aligned text, you have to set the maximum width using ObjText_SetMaxWidth (in order to know where the right border is).

For instance, to align text to the right with a right border at 192 and a maximum width of 300, you can use the following:

ObjText_SetHorizontalAlignment(obj, ALIGNMENT_RIGHT);
ObjText_SetMaxWidth(obj, 300);
ObjRender_SetX(obj, 192 - 300);
