====== ObjText SetFontBorderType ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) border type (real)
Sets the type of the font border among the following:

BORDER_NONE: no border
BORDER_FULL: full border
BORDER_SHADOW: shadow at the bottom right of the text
