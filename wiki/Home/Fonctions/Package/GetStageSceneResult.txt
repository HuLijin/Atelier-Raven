====== GetStageSceneResult ======

Returns the reason the stage was finished.

Return value:
 STAGE_RESULT_CLEARED (real)： Stage was cleared
 STAGE_RESULT_PLAYER_DOWN： Ran out of lives
 STAGE_RESULT_BREAK_OFF： Ended prematurely
