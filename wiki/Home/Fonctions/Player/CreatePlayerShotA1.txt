====== CreatePlayerShotA1 ======

Arguments:
 1) x-coordinate (real)
 2) y-coordinate (real)
 3) speed (real)
 4) angle (real)
 5) damage (real)
 6) penetration power (real)
 7) bullet graphic ID (real)
The player fires the specified shot. If the player is not permitted to shoot, then this function returns void.
