====== GetPlayerState ======

Returns: constant
Gets the player state:

STATE_NORMAL: the player is alive.
STATE_HIT: the player has been hit.
STATE_DOWN: after being killed, before reappearing.
STATE_END: the amount of remaining lives has become 0 (game over).
