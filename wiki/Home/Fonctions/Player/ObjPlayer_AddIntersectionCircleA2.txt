====== ObjPlayer AddIntersectionCircleA2 ======

Parameters:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) grazebox x-coordinate (relative to player object) (real)
 3) grazebox y-coordinate (relative to player object) (real)
 4) graze radius (real)
Creates a grazebox of specified radius for the player object. The grazebox will remain valid for every frame once created.
