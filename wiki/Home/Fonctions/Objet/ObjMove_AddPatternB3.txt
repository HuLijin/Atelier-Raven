====== ObjMove AddPatternB3 ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) frame (real)
 3) x speed (real)
 4) y speed (real)
 5) x acceleration (real)
 6) y acceleration (real)
 7) x max speed (real)
 8) y max speed (real)
 9) bullet graphic (real)
Similar to ObjMove_AddPatternB2. Can also change the bullet graphic. (Replaces SetShotDataA_XY)
