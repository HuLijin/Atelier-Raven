====== ObjMove SetDestAtWeight ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) x coordinate (real)
 3) y coordinate (real)
 4) weight (real)
 5) max speed (real)
Moves the object towards the given coordinates. It will slow down near the end of the movement based on the weight specified.
