====== ObjMove AddPatternA2 ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) frame (real)
 3) speed (real)
 4) angle (real)
 5) acceleration (real)
 6) angular velocity (real)
 7) max speed (real)
Similar to ObjMove_AddPatternA1. Can change object acceleration, angular velocity, and maximum speed. NO_CHANGE can also be used for these new parameters.
