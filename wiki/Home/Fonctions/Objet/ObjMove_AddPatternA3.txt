====== ObjMove AddPatternA3 ======

Arguments:
 1) object ID (real)
 2) frame (real)
 3) speed (real)
 4) angle (real)
 5) acceleration (real)
 6) angular velocity (real)
 7) max speed (real)
 8) bullet graphic (real)
Similar to ObjMove_AddPatternA2. Changes the bullet graphic. (Similar to SetShotDataA, but with acceleration and angular velocity swapped)
