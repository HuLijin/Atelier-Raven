====== CreateShotB2 ======

Arguments:
	1) x-coordinate
	2) y-coordinate
	3) x speed
	4) y speed
	5) x acceleration
	6) y acceleration
	7) x max/min speed
	8) y max/min speed
	9) graphic
	10) delay
Returns:
	Object ID
Same as CreateShotB1, except you can define separate x and y accelerations, and corresponding max/min speeds.
