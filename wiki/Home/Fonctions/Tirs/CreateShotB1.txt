====== CreateShotB1 ======

Arguments:
	1) x-coordinate
	2) y-coordinate
	3) x speed
	4) y speed
	5) graphic
	6) delay
Returns:
	Object ID
Same as CreateShotA1, except you can define separate x and y speeds for the bullet.
