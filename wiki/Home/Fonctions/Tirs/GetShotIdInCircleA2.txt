====== GetShotIdInCircleA2 ======

Arguments:
	1) x-coordinate 
	2) y-coordinate 
	3) radius 
	4) target 
Returns:
	Array
Returns the IDs of the bullets inside the given circle and corresponding to the target in an array.

The target can be:

TARGET_ALL
TARGET_ENEMY
TARGET_PLAYER
