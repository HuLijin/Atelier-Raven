====== GetCurrentDateTimeS ======

Returns a string containing the current date and time. For example, if the current date is 2012/09/16 12:34:56, then "20120916123456" will be returned. If you wanted to convert it to a number, you may use the atoi function; for example,

let year = atoi(GetCurrentDateTimeS[0..4]);
